Thieves Like Us

Thieves Like Us is a one-night art and music pop-up showcasing new work curated and organized by Kim Ruiz. It is an opportunity to see and hear how different artists take inspiration by reinterpreting and repurposing the world around them--stealing.

Thieves Like Us raised over $300 for El Refugio--a local non-profit organization which assists families in visiting their loved ones in immigration detention centers.
